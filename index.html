Safety Equipment & Protection

Safety equipment helps protect workers from hazards, injuries, accidents, and emergencies in the workplace.

1. Fire Extinguisher

Used to control or extinguish small fires during emergencies.

2. Emergency Light

Provides illumination during power outages to help workers evacuate safely during emergencies.

3. First Aid Kit

Contains medical supplies used for treating injuries, cuts, burns, and emergency situations.

4. Safety Helmet

Protects the head from impacts, falling objects, and dangerous workplace hazards.

Evacuation Plan

Follow the designated evacuation routes during emergencies to ensure safety and orderly movement.

Appliances & Maintenance

Proper appliance maintenance helps ensure workplace safety, improves equipment efficiency, and prevents electrical hazards.

1. Air Conditioner

Air conditioning systems help maintain comfortable room temperatures and proper ventilation inside offices and facilities.

Maintenance: Clean filters regularly, inspect electrical connections, and schedule periodic servicing to maintain efficiency.
